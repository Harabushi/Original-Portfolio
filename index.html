About Me

Full Stack web developer leveraging a decade of experience in the mental health field to create a more accessible experience on the web. Moved up to a manager of about 30 people and 5 locations. Recently earned a certificate in full stack development from University of Minnesota Coding, with newly developed skills in Javascript, CSS, HTML, Node.js, the MERN stack, and SQL. I am known as an eager learner, always looking to expand my knowledge base and willing to pick up new skills to complete or improve a project. With each project I aim to improve accessibility and intuitive user experience, as I believe everyone should have access to what the web has to offer. I worked on a team of 4 to develop a MERN app that aims to help people with mental health needs identify and track their mental state on a day by day basis and access resources. I'm excited to use these new skills to help build better experiences on the web for everyone.
